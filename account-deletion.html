How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

Method 1 (In-App):

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"

Method 2 (Web Form):

Submit your request using the form below:

Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account information
  • Game progress and in-game data
  • User activity data linked to the account

Data That May Be Retained

The following data may be retained temporarily:

  • Log data (for analytics and service stability)

Retention Period

Log data is retained for up to 30 days and then automatically deleted.
